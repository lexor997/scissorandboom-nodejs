FAQs

Pricing Questions

The hire rate covers use of the machine for the agreed period. Delivery, fuel (for diesel machines), and damage waiver are priced separately. All machines are delivered fully serviced and safety-checked.

Our minimum hire period is one day. Weekly rates apply for 5-day hires, and we offer discounted 4-week rates for longer projects. Contact us for custom project pricing.

We primarily serve Auckland and the greater Auckland region. Delivery to Waikato, Northland, and other North Island locations can be arranged — please call us to discuss transport costs.

No formal licence is required in NZ to operate a scissor lift or boom lift, but operators must be trained and competent as required under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. We can arrange training through our network of certified trainers on request.

All prices displayed are exclusive of GST. GST at the current NZ rate of 15% will be added to your invoice.
