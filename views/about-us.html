About Scissor & Boom

We're a New Zealand-owned height access equipment rental company built on a simple promise: reliable machines, expert support, and zero compromise on safety.

Who We Are

Safety First, Every Time

At Scissor and Boom, safety is the foundation of everything we do. We understand that working at height carries real risk — and that the right equipment, properly maintained and correctly matched to the task, makes all the difference.

Our fleet of Sinoboom machines is regularly inspected, serviced, and certified. Every hire comes with a pre-delivery check so you know exactly what you're getting. And when you call us with a question, you speak to someone who knows the equipment — not a call centre.

Our Commitment

Any Project, Any Size

No matter the scale or complexity of your project, Scissor and Boom delivers. We've supplied equipment for everything from single-day maintenance jobs to multi-month industrial construction projects across Auckland and beyond.

We work with project managers, sole traders, property companies, government agencies, and event organisers. If you need to work safely at height, we have a machine — and the knowledge to help you choose the right one.
